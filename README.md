I'm a hybrid professional who turned years of logistics coordination and EMEA procurement into a foundation for building data-driven software. By day, I manage global IT procurement at **DraftKings Inc.** — vendor negotiations, inventory forecasting, ISO 27001 compliance. By night, I ship full-stack tools that solve real problems: trend prediction engines, automated job scrapers, and personal knowledge systems.

My formal CS education at **Software University (SoftUni)** gave me JavaScript and SQL.
